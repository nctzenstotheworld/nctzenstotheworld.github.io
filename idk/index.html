To the World, NCT!

NCT stands for Neo Culture Technology, a concept which means the number of members keeps growing. click here to visit nct's sm website

NCT 127

NCT 127 currently consists of 10 members. Taeyong, Johnny, Taeil, Doyoung, Jaehyun, Jungwoo, Mark, Haechan, Yuta, and WinWin. Their latest comeback was released on October 12, 2018. They debuted with 7 members and performed Fire Truck.

NCT U

NCT U doesn't have any fixed members, but the last comeback consisted of Lucas, Taeyong, Jungwoo, Doyoung, Jaehyun, Mark, WinWin, and Ten. The unit debuted with Ten, Mark, Taeil, Taeyong, Doyoung and Jaehyun and released the 7th Sense.

NCT Dream

In NCT Dream, there are currently 7 members. Mark, Renjun, Jeno, Haechan, Jaemin, Chenle and Jisung. Once each member turns 19, they graduate (or leave) NCT dream. Their latest comeback was released in August 2018 and consisted of We Go Up and other tracks.
